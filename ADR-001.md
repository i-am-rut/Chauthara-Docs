# ADR-001: Adopt Domain-Oriented Modular Monolith Backend Architecture

Status: Accepted

## Decision
The backend architecture shall follow a Domain-Oriented Modular Monolith architectural style.

## Rationale
- Preserves approved domain boundaries.
- Supports MVP scope.
- Supports governance boundaries.
- Supports ownership boundaries.
- Minimizes operational complexity.
- Aligns with solo developer constraints.
- Supports future architectural evolution.

## Consequences

### Positive
- Clear domain ownership.
- Low deployment complexity.
- Strong maintainability.
- Evolution-friendly architecture.

### Negative
- Entire application scales as a unit.
- Requires discipline to maintain module boundaries.

### Future Considerations
Modules may be extracted into independent services if justified by measurable operational requirements.
