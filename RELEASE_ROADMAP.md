# Release Roadmap

## Phase 1 — Core Platform

Goal:
Deliver a usable social platform.

Capabilities:

- Registration
- Login
- Profiles
- Following
- Herds
- Posts
- Comments
- Voting
- Feeds
- Image Uploads

Excluded:

- Reporting
- Moderation
- Enforcement
- Governance

Success Criteria:

Users can fully participate in platform discussions.

---

## Phase 2 — Governance & Operations

Goal:
Introduce governance.

Capabilities:

- Reporting
- Moderation
- Enforcement
- Escalation
- Governance workflows

Success Criteria:

Platform can self-govern.

---

## Future

- Background Processing
- Observability
- Advanced Operations
- Advanced Scaling
